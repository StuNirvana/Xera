XERA — Event Horizon

Event Horizon is engineered as a daily-wear instrument. At its core is the VH31 smooth-sweep quartz movement — chosen for its continuous seconds motion, accuracy, shock resistance, and slimmer case profile. The result is fluid visual movement without unnecessary thickness.

The case is machined from 316L stainless steel and finished in a uniform bead blast. The matte surface removes glare and visual noise, creating a restrained, cold-engineered aesthetic that allows the dial to remain the focal point.

A flat sapphire crystal sits flush above the case. Distortion-free and scratch resistant, it preserves the geometry of the luminous ring with absolute clarity.

The strap is premium FKM rubber. Denser and more resilient than standard silicone, FKM resists UV, moisture, and temperature variation while maintaining structural integrity and comfort on the wrist.

The engraved caseback completes the construction — precise, permanent, and intentional.

Every material and finish has been selected to support the concept without distraction.

Event Horizon is deliberate. Built to be worn daily. Built to endure.
