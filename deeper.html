XERA — Event Horizon

Event Horizon is engineered as a daily-wear instrument. At its core is the VH31 smooth-sweep quartz movement — chosen for its continuous seconds motion, accuracy, shock resistance, and slimmer case profile.

The case is machined from 316L stainless steel and finished in a uniform bead blast. The matte surface removes glare and visual noise, creating a restrained, cold-engineered aesthetic.

A flat sapphire crystal sits flush above the case.

The strap is premium FKM rubber.

The engraved caseback completes the construction.

Event Horizon is deliberate. Built to be worn daily. Built to endure.
